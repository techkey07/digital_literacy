Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

Prevention Tips:
1. Use strong passwords for your email, social media, and banking apps.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
3. Do not share personal information such as Aadhaar number, OTP, or passwords with anyone online.
4. Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
5. Download apps only from trusted sources like the Play Store or App Store.
6. Be careful when using public Wi-Fi and avoid logging into banking or payment apps on unsecured networks.
7. UPI Safety Tip: Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
8. UPI Safety Tip: Always check whether you are sending money or approving a collect request before entering your UPI PIN.
9. Regularly check your bank and UPI transaction alerts to detect unauthorized activity.
10. Log out of accounts when using shared computers in libraries or computer labs.

If you become a victim of cyber fraud:
Report immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
