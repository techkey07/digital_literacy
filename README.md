

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio created as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. The objective of this project is to develop an understanding of digital literacy and to learn how digital tools, online platforms, and communication methods can be used responsibly and effectively in academic and professional environments. The project consists of five tasks, each related to a specific module of the course, and together they demonstrate practical knowledge of digital awareness, professional communication, collaboration tools, and cyber safety.

The first task involved creating a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic. For this task, I designed a one-page visual infographic using a design tool to explain the meaning and importance of digital literacy. The infographic highlights topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence. The aim was to present important information in a simple and visually engaging way so that students can easily understand the importance of digital literacy.

The second task focused on building a Student Digital Portfolio. In this task, I created or updated professional accounts on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and other learning platforms. These platforms help students showcase their projects, build a professional identity, and connect with academic and industry communities. Screenshots of the profiles have been included in the repository as proof of the accounts created.

The third task involved exploring Coding and Collaboration Platforms. I created an account on a coding practice platform and completed a beginner-level programming challenge to understand how such platforms help improve problem-solving skills. In addition, I created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms. The quiz includes five questions related to digital literacy concepts and demonstrates how online tools can be used for collaboration, surveys, and academic activities.link to the quiz: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1m3Cki8XclCl8_XLuerCAZ_0V_xCNCDve1-Xjf0sIbok/edit#responses

The fourth task focused on Professional Email Communication and Social Media Etiquette. I drafted two professional emails: one requesting an assignment extension from a professor and another expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity. Both emails follow proper email etiquette including a clear subject line, professional greeting, structured message, and appropriate closing. I also created a checklist describing responsible social media practices for college students.

The fifth task covered Cybercrime Awareness. In this task, I researched a cybercrime scenario and wrote a case study explaining how the cybercrime occurs, who is typically targeted, and what the consequences can be. I also created a “Stay Safe Online” checklist that provides practical tips for students to avoid online fraud and cyber threats. The checklist also includes official reporting channels such as the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and the cybercrime helpline number 1930.

Overall, this project helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in today’s digital world. Through these tasks, I learned how to create a professional digital presence, use online collaboration tools, communicate responsibly, and stay safe while using the internet. The repository contains all related screenshots, documents, and the final project report that together demonstrate my learning from this course.
