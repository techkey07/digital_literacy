| **Do’s**                                                               | **Don’ts**                                                       |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Think before posting any content online.                               | Do not share personal or private information publicly.            |
| Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments.              | Do not spread rumors or false information.                        |
| Protect your accounts with strong passwords and privacy settings.      | Do not engage in cyberbullying or harassment.                     |
| Verify information before sharing it with others.                      | Do not post inappropriate or offensive content.                   |
| Use social media for learning, networking, and positive communication. | Do not spend excessive time on social media that affects studies. |
